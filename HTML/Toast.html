Peanut Butter Toast + Protien Shake

A sweet breakfast paired with a protein shake, allowing it to fulfill your sweet tooth and also meet your goals.
Recipe ⇣
  • 1 or 2 slices of bread (as per choice)
  • Smooth peanut butter (low fat preferrable)
  • 1 scoop protein powder (plant-based or whey)
  1. Toast the slices of bread and smear 5-10g of peanut butter on them.
  2. Take 250ml of water and add 1 scoop of the protein powder. Shake well.
Nutritional Content ⇣
Calories: 180 kcal~
Protein: 29.4 g~
Carbohydrate: 28.9 g~
Fat: 4.3 g~
