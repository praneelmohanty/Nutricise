Healthy Chicken Chaap

A very tasty take on Chaap created using a variety of rich spices and masalas.
Recipe ⇣
  • 1 whole leg of chicken
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 grated tomato
  • 4 tsp poppy seeds and almonds paste
  • 1/4 cup curd
  • 1/8 cup milk
  • 1/8 cup roasted gram flour
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 green chilli chopped
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2tsp rose water
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 bay Leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 pod cardamoms
  • 3 pepper corns
  • 3 tsp coriander chopped
  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add fennel seeds, pepper corns, bay Leaf, cloves and cardamom.
  2. Once they splutter add onion and salt and fry till they get brown and soft.
  3. Add the ginger garlic paste and saute till the raw aroma goes away.
  4. Add chilli, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder and salt and saute for 2 minutes.
  5. Add 1/4 cup water and cook the spices for 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Add tomatoes and cook till it gets soft and mushy.
  7. Add chicken and saute in the masala for 5 minutes.
  8. Add in poppy almond paste, gram flour, milk and yogurt and mix well.
  9. Fry the gravy till the oil starts to leave sides, for around 5 minutes and then add garam masala and stir well.
  10. Add 1/2 cup of water, cover the pan and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  11. Uncover the pan and cook till the chicken is completely done.
  12. Finally, add the rose water and switch off the flame.
  13. Garnish with coriander and serve hot
