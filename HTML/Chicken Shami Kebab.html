Chicken Shami Kebab

This high nutritional chicken curry is packed with protein and taste. Definitely proves how you can make tasty as well as healthy food.
Recipe ⇣
  • 200g boneless chicken cubes
  • 1/4 cup chickpeas soaked overnight
  • 2 dry red chili
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp whole coriander
  • 1 clove
  • 2 black peppercorn
  • 1/2 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/4 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tbsp chopped mint leaves
  • 1 chopped green chilli
  • 1/2 inch roughly chopped ginger
  • 2 chopped cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  1. Heat 1/2 tbsp ghee in a kadai (wok) and add the whole spices and let them splutter.
  2. Add ginger garlic and saute till the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add the chicken, salt, and turmeric and saute for 2 mins.
  4. Then add coriander, mint, green chilies and chickpeas and saute for more 10 mins.
  5. Add 2 cups of water and boil the mixture till the chicken and chickpeas are cooked and the water is evaporated.
  6. Allow the mixture to cool down completely.
  7. Then add the egg and grind the mixture to a smooth paste.
  8. Wet your hands and make round shape kebabs of the mixture.
  9. Pan fry the kebabs in the remaining ghee.
  10. Serve hot with mint chutney and onion rings.
