Paneer Cheese Taco

This fusion of Indian and Mexican cuisine is a finger-licker as paneer full of protein is wrapped in a taco-shaped paratha
Recipe ⇣
  • 100g Paneer
  • 10g grated cheese
  • 50g wheat flour
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  1. Using the wheat flour, make one paratha by using olive oil.
  2. Cut the paneer into small cubes and place them inside the paratha as filling. Grate some cheese on top of that.
  3. On a pan, at heat, put a drizzle of olive oil and toss the filled paratha till cooked and slightly golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with any sauce of liking.
