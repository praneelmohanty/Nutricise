Egg Breakfast Burrito

A Mexican dish filled with rich protein and nutricious ingredients to fulfill your morning cravings as well as to meet your protein intake goal.
Recipe ⇣
  • 3 eggs
  • 60g wheat flour
  • 50g boiled black beans
  • 1tbsp salsa
  • 1 finely diced tomato
  • 10g grated cheese
  1. Using the wheat flour, prepare a large roti/tortilla.
  2. Break the eggs into a heating pan, add salt and scramble properly.
  3. Lay the tortilla down and spread the salsa.
  4. Put the beans, then the scrambbled eggs and lastly the the tomatoes on top.
  5. Grate cheese on this and lastly add a bit of salsa if required.
  6. Lastly, in a heating pan, toast the burrito and enjoy!
