Pepper Paneer Curry

This high nutritional paneer curry is packed with protein and taste. Definitely proves how you can make tasty as well as healthy food.
Recipe ⇣
  • 200g diced paneer
  • 1/2 diced onion
  • 1/2 green chilli (optional)
  • 2 sliced garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 sprig curry leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/8 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 clove
  • 3tsp olive oil
  • 3tsp crushed pepper
  • 1/8 coriander leaves
  1. Season the paneer pieces with turmeric powder, ginger garlic paste, salt and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, mix and keep this in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan, temper the oil with cumin, cinnamon stick, cloves and 3/4 portion of curry leaves.
  3. Add sliced garlic, onions and cook till they turn golden.
  4. Add green chillies and the seasoned paneer pieces, sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Add coriander powder and crushed pepper, mix well and cook on low flame until the oil gets separated, stir and sprinkle some water if required.
  6. Add 1/4 cup water, once water will start boiling, reduce the heat and cover with the lid. Cook until the paneer is done.
  7. Once the paneer is cooked open the lid and cook till the moisture evaporates.
  8. Sprinkle chopped 3/4 th of the coriander leaves and the remaining curry leaves.
  9. Before serving, garnish with 1 teaspoon of Lemon Juice and the remaining coriander leaves.
  10. Serve with 1 cup rice or 2 medium-sized atta rotis
