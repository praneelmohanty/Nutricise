Veg Breakfast Burrito

A Mexican dish filled with rich Indian-based protein and nutricious ingredients to fulfill your morning cravings as well as to meet your protein intake goal.
Recipe ⇣
  • 50g diced paneer
  • 50g boiled kidney Beans
  • 50g boiled black beans
  • 60g wheat flour
  • 1tbsp salsa
  • 1 finely diced tomato
  • 10g grated cheese
  1. Using the wheat flour, prepare a large roti/tortilla.
  2. Lay the tortilla down and spread the salsa.
  3. Put the black and kidney beans and then on top add diced paneer. Lastly, put the tomatoe on top.
  4. Grate cheese on this and lastly add a bit of salsa if required.
  5. Lastly, in a heating pan, toast the burrito and enjoy!
