High Protein Seed Mix

This high protein seed mix is rich in essential macro as well as micro-nutrients.
Recipe ⇣
  • 100g chickpeas
  • 50g sprouts
  • 100g kidney beans
  • salt to taste
  • 50g peanuts
  • 10g ginger
  • 1 finely chopped chilli
  • 1 curry leaf
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  1. Keep the seeds in water overnight
  2. Meanwhile, take the peanuts and roast for a while.
  3. Cool down the peanuts and then grind this to make the peanut chutney to add taste to your seed mix.
  4. Take ginger, chilli and curry leaf along with olive oil and put them in a mixer along with a little water. Put some salt according to taste and some tamarind.
  5. The next day, put the seeds in a pressure cooker for 2 whistles to go off.
  6. Put all of the ingredients together in a bowl and use the peanut chutney as a side.
