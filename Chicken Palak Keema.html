Palak Chicken Keema

This high nutritional chicken curry is packed with protein and taste. Definitely proves how you can make tasty as well as healthy food.
Recipe ⇣
  • 200g minced chicken
  • 150g spinach (palak)
  • /2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 bay leaf
  • 3 peppercorn
  • 2 clovese
  • 1/2 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 finely chopped green chilli
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  1. Take a bunch of palak and finely chop them.
  2. Heat a pan and once hot add oil, bay leaf, peppercorn, cloves, cinnamon. Let them crackle.
  3. Add onion, ginger garlic paste, green chilies, salt.
  4. Sauté for 2/3 minutes till the onions are translucent.
  5. Cook until water evaporates and add minced chicken. Mix well.
  6. Sauté till keema dries up and then add water. Mix once and put the lid on.
  7. Let it cook with the lid on. Once cooked add garam masala and mix.
  8. Garnish with coriander and serve with brown roti or rice.
    9. Note: Keep mixing to avoid the formation of meat lumps.
