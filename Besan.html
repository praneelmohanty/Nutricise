High Protein Besan Pancake/Chilla

This high protein chicken curry is packed with protein and taste. Proves how you can make tasty as well as healthy food.
Recipe ⇣
  • 100g besan/chickpea flour
  • 25g boiled chickpeas
  • 25g boiled lentils
  • 25g boiled green gram
  • 25g boiled black gram
  • chopped green chilli (optional)
  • finely chopped coriander
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ghee oil
  • salt to taste
  1. Then in a mixer, add the gram flour, the seeds and a little water to create a batter.
  2. To the batter, add the coriander, chilli, salt, masala according to taste and 2tsp of olive oil. Then mix the batter till a thick consistency
  3. Take a pan and use a ladle to give the pancake(chilla) its circular shape. Drizzle ghee or olive oil on the batter.
  4. Paneer can be added as an optional topping.
  5. Serve hot with any chutney or sauce.
