Egg Cheese Sandwich

A simple egg cheese sandwich which provides you with a ton of Protein, quality nutrients and taste.
Recipe ⇣
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 slices of brown bread
  • 10g grated cheese
  • 1 tsp butter
  • salt to taste
  1. Break the eggs and make one omelette using them, use salt as required.
  2. Cut out one quarter of the omelette and put it one of the slices and the rest in the other 2 to make a sandwich. Put the cheese on the insides of these sandwiches.
  3. Heat a pan and add 1tsp of butter. Toss the sandwiches around until slightly golden brown and the cheese inside has melt.
  4. Serve hot with any sauce of liking.
